Mafia

Mafia is the ultimate test of your cunning and manipulative skills. In this game, deception is your best weapon. You have to be sly, lie to your friends, wear different masks, and orchestrate betrayals without getting caught. Can you survive the psychological warfare?

Key Features

  • Intense psychological gameplay based on deception.
  • Real-time multiplayer lobbies to manipulate your friends.
  • Blind role distribution for maximum unpredictability.
  • Atmospheric UI designed for a dark, secretive experience.

Technologies Used

Flutter Dart Firebase UI/UX AI
